Drone with AI of Situations

Project information

  • Category: Hardware/Software
  • Project date: 01 july, 2022

The project idea is to design an AI-controlled drone based on computer vision response. The original concept is to design a drone with embedded AI capable of distinguishing risky situations such as assaults, fights, accidents, among others, and taking action after analysis, whether to inform authorities or instructing the injured on the steps to take, like a type of intelligent and mobile camera. I've already made great progress with this idea; now I can control the drone through a computer, and together with my team, we've developed an AI capable of identifying possible dangerous items such as knives or firearms. The project is still in development; we need to go through many tests and refinements until a viable version, but it's already very promising. Moreover, the concept of analyzing situations is very broad and can be applied in various areas, such as customs inspections, searching for victims in disaster zones, agriculture, or livestock, for example, searching for lost cattle, behavioral analysis of specific individuals, among others.
